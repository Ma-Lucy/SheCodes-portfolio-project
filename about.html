ABOUT

I love lifestyle photography... I have a passion for finding beauty in my surroundings, and I find taking my camera forces me to look up, stop and fully appreciate the location I am in. I balance my passion for photography and curating my online print collections with a deep love of health and wellness.

I moved to the UK in 2019 to continue my photography work, but have been fortunate enough to travel widely through Australia, Asia, New Zealand, India, Canada and America. I believe these different cultures and landscapes developed my interest in photography, providing me an opportunity to capture beauty in vastly different environments. Through photography I hope to travel the globe, while bringing amazing and beautiful locations to life through my photographic prints.
